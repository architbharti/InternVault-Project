About the Internship
The Backend Developer Internship at Zomato offers a chance to contribute to the infrastructure that powers one of India’s leading food delivery platforms. Interns will work on designing and building scalable RESTful APIs, handling databases, and improving the reliability and speed of backend systems. You'll work with technologies like Node.js, Python, MySQL, MongoDB, Redis, Kafka, and cloud platforms like AWS or GCP. Interns will participate in microservices architecture, database design, authentication systems, and performance tuning of APIs. You'll work in Agile teams, contribute to daily stand-ups, and participate in debugging, deployment, and version control using Git. Mentorship from experienced engineers and real-world projects provide a solid foundation in server-side development, data handling, and service reliability. This is an ideal opportunity to learn scalable backend development and impact real-world use cases at scale.
